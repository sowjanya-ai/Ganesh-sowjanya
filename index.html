Our Story

The Groom & Bride

❀
Ganesh

Ganesh Pasupunoori

Gentle by nature and calm in spirit, Ganesh is the kind of soul who notices what others overlook — and quietly does something about it. An introvert with a heart that listens deeply, he understands without ever judging. He is my water reminder, my steady presence, my most patient audience. We met in the most unexpected of ways, yet here we are — two strangers who somehow found home in each other. 🌿

Sowjanya

Sowjanya Kambala

Warm, expressive, and full of heart — Sowjanya is the kind of person who makes every room feel a little brighter. She loves deeply, laughs freely, and brings an energy that is impossible to ignore. Thoughtful in her words and genuine in her care, she is someone who turns ordinary moments into something beautiful. If love had a form, a voice, a soul - it would be her. Always her 🌸
